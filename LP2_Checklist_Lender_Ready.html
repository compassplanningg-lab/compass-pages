What's inside

Organized exactly the way lenders think

Most business owners show up with some documents but not all. Missing one item can stall — or kill — an approval. This checklist closes that gap.

Financial statements

P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow — last 2–3 years. Exactly what format lenders want and why CPA-reviewed is better.

Tax records

Business and personal returns for 2–3 years. Required by virtually every SBA and bank lender — no exceptions.

Cash flow documentation

12-month forecast plus bank statements — how lenders confirm you can actually repay.

Ownership & legal docs

Entity documents, ownership structure, licenses, and permits. Lenders verify your business is legally sound.

Collateral & debt schedule

What you own and what you owe. Lenders calculate your total debt load before deciding how much more to extend.

Your financial narrative

Use of funds statement and explanations for unusual items. Proactive clarity builds lender trust — surprises destroy it.
